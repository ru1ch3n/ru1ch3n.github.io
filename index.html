Introduction

I work at the intersection of AI for Science and AI for Finance, focusing on neural operators, physics-guided diffusion, and RL-guided optimization for PDE-constrained systems. I care about reliability and reproducibility, and I run large-scale experiments on Seawulf/NVwulf HPC clusters.

Recent directions include Adaptive PDE-Observation Diffusion (APOD) for physics-consistent sampling, the RL-QESA framework that enforces quasi-equilibrium during simulated annealing, benchmarks for sparse/partial observation PDEs, and analyses of discretization mismatch and expressivity in Fourier Neural Operators.
