Introduction

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computational Applied Mathematics at Stony Brook University. My research focuses on machine learning for PDEs, neural operators, and stochastic optimization. I work on data-efficient operator-learning frameworks and optimization strategies (e.g., simulated annealing guided by reinforcement learning) to improve cross-resolution generalization and computational performance in scientific modeling.
